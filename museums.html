The historic pink palace in Tahrir Square, containing the world's most extensive collection of pharaonic antiquities spanning 5,000 years.

Luxor temple

nlike many other temples in Egypt, Luxor Temple was not built to worship a specific god or a deceased pharaoh. Instead, it was primarily dedicated to the rejuvenation of kingship and was the site where many kings of Egypt may have been crowned.
